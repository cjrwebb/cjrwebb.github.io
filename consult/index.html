Consultancy and Training

Posted at — May 9, 2021

I have over five years of experience as an academic teacher and in advisory and consultant roles for projects that span research, public administration, logistics, monitoring and evaluation. I bring practical data science skills and cutting-edge analytical methods from academic research to client projects.

My research has been published in world-leading peer-reviewed journals and my data analytics applications are used globally.

You can view a portfolio of recent data visualisation projects; a fully interactive data visualisation and analysis web application I created that has been used by every local authority in England; and details of my academic research publications, to get a sense of my skills and specialisation.

What I do

  • Bespoke, high-quality, small-scale research projects, static or interactive data visualisation, and statistical analyses that make use of existing secondary data.
  • Specialist training in statistical methods, data science, and data analytics using R, SPSS, or Mplus, especially for structural equation modelling and multilevel modelling.
  • Advisory work on research methodology, methods, and design.
  • Data wrangling.

What I do not (generally) do

  • Standalone data cleaning and web scraping.
  • Primary data collection.
  • Data warehousing, continuous delivery, automated ML pipelines, database server architecture, or other forms of information engineering.
